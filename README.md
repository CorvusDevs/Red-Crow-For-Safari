Red Crow transforms your YouTube experience in Safari with 40+ customizable features. Fine-tune playback speed, quality, volume, and more — all without leaving your browser. Red Crow is not a Chrome extension ported to Safari. It's built specifically for Safari with native performance, no background processes, and zero data collection.

## Features

- **Speed Control** — Set any default speed from 0.25x to 16x, with per-channel memory.
- **Player Toolbar** — 19 in-player buttons: screenshot, loop, A-B loop, Picture-in-Picture, cinema mode, pop-out, rotate, flip, and more.
- **SponsorBlock** — Automatically skip sponsors, intros, outros, self-promotion, and filler segments.
- **Hide Distractions** — Remove comments, related videos, Shorts, end cards, notifications, ads, and visual clutter.
- **Return of Dislikes** — Restore dislike counts and the like/dislike ratio bar on every video.
- **DeArrow** — Replace clickbait titles and thumbnails with community-submitted alternatives.
- **Video Filters** — Adjust brightness, contrast, saturation, blur, warmth, sepia, and hue in real time.
- **Custom Themes** — Nord, Solarized Dark, Catppuccin Mocha, plus forced light/dark theme overrides.
- **Theater & Cinema Mode** — Auto-enter theater mode, dim the page background, or go fully cinematic.
- **Volume Boost** — Amplify audio up to 600% beyond YouTube's limit. Scroll over the player to change volume.
- **Channel Profiles** — Save per-channel settings for speed, quality, volume, and cinema mode.
- **Keyboard Shortcuts** — Customizable shortcuts for loop, screenshot, PiP, cinema mode, and every feature.
- **Mini-Player** — A floating mini-player that follows you as you scroll down the page.
- **Chapter Navigation** — A slide-out panel showing all chapters for quick seeking.
- **Blocked Channels & Duration Filter** — Hide videos from channels you don't want to see, or outside your preferred length range.
- **Usage Timer** — Get reminded to take a break after a configurable watch time.
- **Presets & Custom CSS** — One-click configurations (Podcast, Music, Minimal, Focus) plus full CSS injection.

## Privacy

No account required. No sign-ups, no cloud sync, no tracking. Your settings live entirely on your device. Red Crow collects zero data.
