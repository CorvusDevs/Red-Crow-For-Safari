Red Crow transforms your YouTube experience in Safari with over 50 customizable features. Fine-tune playback speed, quality, volume, and more — all without leaving your browser.

HOW IT WORKS
Install the extension, open YouTube, and everything works automatically with sensible defaults. Open the settings page to customize every detail, or use quick presets to apply popular configurations in one click.

KEY FEATURES

- Custom playback speed — Set any default speed from 0.25x to 16x, with per-channel memory.
- Quality control — Force a default quality and a separate fullscreen quality.
- Volume boost — Amplify audio up to 600% beyond YouTube's limit.
- Theater & cinema mode — Auto-enter theater mode, dim the page background, or go fully cinematic.
- SponsorBlock — Automatically skip sponsors, intros, outros, and other non-content segments.
- DeArrow — Replace clickbait titles and thumbnails with community-sourced alternatives.
- Hide distractions — Remove comments, related videos, Shorts, end cards, notifications, and more.
- Custom themes — Choose from Nord, Solarized Dark, Catppuccin Mocha, or force YouTube's own light/dark theme.
- Player toolbar — Screenshot, loop, A-B loop, picture-in-picture, reverse playback, and more — all from an in-player toolbar.
- Video filters — Adjust brightness, contrast, saturation, blur, hue, and more in real time.
- Scroll volume — Change volume by scrolling over the player.
- Chapter navigation — A slide-out panel showing all chapters for quick seeking.
- Channel profiles — Save per-channel settings for speed, quality, volume, and cinema mode.
- Usage timer — Get reminded to take a break after a configurable watch time.
- Keyboard shortcuts — Customizable shortcuts for loop, screenshot, PiP, cinema, and more.
- Mini-player — A floating mini-player that follows you as you scroll down the page.
- Blocked channels — Filter out videos from channels you don't want to see.
- Duration filter — Hide videos shorter or longer than your preferred range.
- Presets — One-click configurations for Podcast, Presentation, Music, Minimal, and Focus modes.
- Custom CSS — Inject your own styles for complete visual control.

SETTINGS & CUSTOMIZATION
The full settings page is organized into clear sections with a search bar and setting descriptions. Import, export, or reset your configuration at any time.

PRIVACY FIRST
Red Crow does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. It runs entirely on your device with no analytics, no tracking, and no accounts required. Your browsing stays private.
